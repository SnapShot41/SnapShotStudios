The Heart behind the Camera

I’m Burleigh‑Cleaver — Shooter1 — the eye behind Snap Shot Studios. Thirty‑two years as a medic taught me how to stay steady when the world gets loud, how to read a room in seconds, and how to catch the details most people miss. When I hung up the scrubs, I didn’t slow down — I shifted gears. I chased the creative fire I’d been carrying for years and earned my degree in Commercial Photography. Now I bring that same intensity, precision, and instinct into every frame I shoot. I’m drawn to the moments that hit different — the quiet ones, the chaotic ones, the ones that punch you right in the chest. Fast‑moving scenes, raw emotion, real stories unfolding in real time… that’s where I live. My goal is simple: make every snap feel unmistakably you. Bold. Honest. Unfiltered. Art with a pulse.
